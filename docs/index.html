Email that stays out of your way

A calmer, local-first desktop email, calendar and address book client. It connects straight to your own IMAP or POP3 mailbox and keeps a local, searchable copy for offline use, with no PigeonPost cloud or account in between.

Windows today; macOS and Linux builds planned.

What it does

The everyday email workflows that carry daily use, without the clutter.

📥

IMAP and POP3

Connect standard mailboxes with a guided or manual setup. Multiple accounts, each with its own separate inbox.

🗂️

Familiar three-pane

Folders, message list and an optional reading pane (F8 to toggle). Sort the list by date, group it into conversations and see the To and Cc correspondents on open. Select many messages with Ctrl or Shift and delete, mark, star or move them at once, with a right-click menu and reader tabs.

✉️

Compose and attach

Rich-text mail with To, Cc and Bcc, per-account signatures, reply, reply-all and forward. Attach files or forward an existing email as an attachment. A local autosave offers your draft back after an accidental close.

📎

Attachments

A paperclip flags mail that carries attachments. Open the message to see each file with its size and save it wherever you like; the bytes are cached, so saving works offline.

🏷️

Tags, flags and badges

Mark read or unread, star what matters, label messages with colour, mark junk to the Junk folder and see unread counts per folder and account.

🗃️

Folders and rules

Create, rename and delete folders in a nested tree. Filter rules mark or flag mail on arrival, matching From, To, Cc or Subject.

📤

Works offline

Read cached mail with no connection. Sends written offline queue in a per-account outbox and deliver automatically on reconnect.

🔒

Local-first and private

Mail is cached and searchable offline, remote images are blocked by default and passwords stay in your OS keychain.

📅

Calendar and reminders

Month, week and day views. Recurring events with their own time zone and reminders that pop an on-screen banner. Import and export .ics files that round-trip with Outlook and Thunderbird.

📇

Address book

Keep contacts locally with vCard and CSV import and export, so they round-trip with Outlook and Thunderbird.

🤝

Meeting invites

Send and receive meeting invitations with Accept, Tentative and Decline. A Teams, Meet, Zoom or Webex join link opens in your browser.

🔔

Instant new mail

New mail is pushed the moment it arrives over IMAP IDLE, with a desktop notification even when the window is hidden to the tray.

Coming next: macOS and Linux builds, one-click Microsoft sign-in and two-way CalDAV and CardDAV sync.

Built with care

A clean, testable core and a single small binary per platform.
